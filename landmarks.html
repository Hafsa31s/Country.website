Famous Landmarks
of Turkey

Hagia Sophia

Hagia Sophia, meaning "Holy Wisdom," is a historic building in Istanbul, Turkey, that has served as a cathedral,
mosque, and museum, and is now a mosque again. Originally built between 532 and 537 AD.

Cappadocia

Famous for its unique rock formations and hot-air balloons.Cappadocia is famous for traditional cave hotels.
The area is a popular tourist destination, as it has many areas with unique geological, historic, cultural, and religious features.

Ephesus

Ephesus was a major ancient Greek and Roman city in modern-day Turkey, renowned as a center for trade, religion, and culture.
