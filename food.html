Turkish Food

Kebabs

Turkish food is a rich, diverse cuisine featuring fresh ingredients like lamb, eggplant, tomatoes, yogurt, and spices, known for iconic dishes such as savory kebabs (Döner, Adana), flaky pastries (Börek), stuffed vegetables (Dolma), fresh dips (Hummus, Babaganoush), hearty breakfasts (Menemen).

Turkish Delight (Lokum)

Turkish delight tastes sweet and is chewy, with a variety of flavors like rosewater, lemon, and pistachio being common. Its texture is soft and gelatinous, similar to a gummy candy but less firm, and it is often dusted with powdered sugar or cornstarch.

Turkish Tea

Turkish tea is special due to its strong, bold flavor, unique preparation method using a double teapot called a çaydanlık, and its deep cultural significance in Turkey.
