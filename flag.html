Flag of Turkey

The Turkish flag features a bright red background with a white crescent moon and five-pointed star.

The red color symbolizes bravery and strength, while the crescent and star have deep historical ties to Turkish culture. The modern flag design was officially adopted in 1844 during the late Ottoman period and continued as the national flag after the founding of the Republic of Turkey.
